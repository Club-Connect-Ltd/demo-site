# Brand Typography

## Font Families

### Oxanium (Primary/Display)
- **Role:** Logo, impact text, large pullouts, numbers, graphical elements, headlines, scores
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700), ExtraBold (800)
- **Character:** Bold, geometric, slightly rounded. Confident and modern.
- **Usage:** Headlines, navigation titles, match scores, stat numbers, hero sections

### Poppins (Secondary/Body)
- **Role:** Body text, UI labels, subtitles, captions, form fields, buttons
- **Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
- **Character:** Clean geometric sans-serif. Friendly and highly readable.
- **Usage:** Body copy, button labels, form inputs, list items, captions, error text

### League Spartan (Deprecated)
- Kept for backward compatibility only
- All new development should use Oxanium or Poppins

## Design Sentiment
The typography pairing reflects Club Connect's brand personality:
- **Oxanium** brings energy and sportiness — it feels dynamic, modern, like a scoreboard or match graphic
- **Poppins** brings warmth and accessibility — friendly enough for parents, clean enough for coaches
- Together they create a sporty-yet-approachable feel that matches a community sports app
